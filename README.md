# 🛡️ UAE E-Commerce Fraud Risk Analysis Dashboard

## 📝 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing fraud risks in UAE-based e-commerce transactions using **Excel Dashboarding, Data Analysis, and Business Intelligence techniques**.

The dashboard analyzes over **100,000 transaction records** to identify fraud patterns, risky payment behaviors, suspicious shipping activities, and high-risk customer segments.

The project helps businesses:

* Detect fraud-prone transactions
* Reduce revenue loss
* Improve payment security
* Strengthen fraud prevention strategies
* Make data-driven operational decisions

# 📌 Dashboard Insights

## 1️⃣ Fraud by Risk Level

* Medium-risk transactions account for the largest share of transactions.
* High-risk transactions contribute significantly to fraud activity.
* Very High-Risk transactions are minimal but critical to monitor.

### Key Observation

The overall fraud rate of **8.21%** is significantly above the industry average of **1–2%**.

---

## 2️⃣ Payment Method Fraud Analysis

* Card payments show the highest fraud volume compared to:

  * Apple Pay
  * Google Pay
  * Bank Transfer
  * PayPal

### Business Impact

Card-based transactions require stronger authentication mechanisms such as:

* CVV validation
* 3D Secure (3DS2)
* Transaction velocity checks

---

## 3️⃣ Fraud by Time & Hour

* Fraud activity is significantly higher during daytime hours.
* Peak fraud windows occur during business hours to imitate legitimate customer behavior.

### Recommendation

Deploy:

* Real-time fraud monitoring
* Hour-based alert systems
* Additional authentication during peak-risk hours

---

## 4️⃣ Product Category Risk Analysis

High fraud-prone categories include:

* Beauty
* Digital Goods
* Electronics

### Key Insight

Digital products are especially vulnerable because they involve instant fulfillment without physical verification.

---

## 5️⃣ Address Match Analysis

* Transactions with billing and shipping address mismatches show higher fraud probability.
* Around **7%+** of transactions contain mismatched addresses.

### Recommendation

Flag all mismatch transactions for:

* Manual review
* OTP verification
* Additional customer authentication

---

## 6️⃣ IP Risk Analysis

The dashboard categorizes customer IP addresses into:

* Low Risk
* Medium Risk
* High Risk

### Key Observation

High-risk IP traffic should be automatically blocked or restricted using:

* IP reputation APIs
* Geo-location validation
* Device fingerprinting

---

## 7️⃣ Shipping City Risk Analysis

The dashboard identifies suspicious shipping destinations including:

* UAE cities
* International locations such as:

  * Cairo
  * Karachi
  * Mumbai
  * Manama

### Business Impact

Cross-border shipping can indicate:

* Stolen card activity
* Fake customer identities
* High chargeback probability

# 📈 Business Recommendations

| Priority    | Recommendation                                          |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| 🔴 Critical | Reduce fraud rate below 2% using ML-based fraud scoring |
| 🔴 Critical | Implement 3DS2 authentication for card payments         |
| 🟠 High     | Auto-flag billing and shipping mismatches               |
| 🟠 High     | Add OTP verification for high-risk product categories   |
| 🟡 Medium   | Deploy daytime fraud monitoring alerts                  |
| 🟡 Medium   | Implement IP reputation scoring                         |
